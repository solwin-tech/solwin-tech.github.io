What We Do

Pediatric Service

A child speciality Hospital offering utmost tender loving child care and quality treatment by dedicated doctor (Consultant Paediatrician and Neonatologist) available round the clock 24 × 7 for emergency services. It is an 18 bedded hospital, associated with Gayatri Nursing Home.
Holistic child care in health and illness is our motto. We offer affordable, trustworthy and professional service to ensure your best health. We provide all types of vaccination, nutritional counselling, and comprehensive management of neonatal and children problems. Ours is technological advanced Hospital having facility of prescription on digital platform; online appointment and computerized records. The Child Specialist

Dr Prashant Khadayate

Director of Paediatric Unit

A hard working, compassionate, and responsible Paediatrician.

Introduction

Dr Prashant Khadayate completed M.B.B.S and D.Ch (Ped) from Gandhi Medical College & associated Hamidia, Kamla Nehru & Sultania Zanana Hospitals; under Barkatullah University, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh in June 2009. Dr Prashant Khadayate has worked at Synergy Pediatric Hospital, Maninagar, Ahmedabad; Miracles Children Hospital, Bhopal; Aashirvaad superspeciality Children Hospital, Vadodara and The Children Hospital, Vadodara as Consultant Pediatrician & Neonatal Intensivist. Dr Prashant Khadayate is well experienced with special interest in Vaccinology, Nutrition and Infectious Diseases.

Academic Advancements :

  • Successfully completed provider course in Paediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS) organized by Indian Academy of Paediatrics in accordance with the standard of American Heart Association, November 2008.
  • Participated in Paediatric Cardiology workshop (2007), at Department of Pediatrics, Gandhi Medical College, Bhopal.
  • Trained under Art and Science Of Vaccinology (2012), Safe Injection Practices (2013), RTI-GEM (2013) under “IAP Action Plan2012” held by Indian Academy of Paediatrics, Bhopal branch.
  • Participated in Allergic Rhinitis and Co-Morbidities Workshop (2013) organised by Indian Academy of Paediatrics; Allergy and Applied Immunology Chapter, Bhopal.
  • Successfully completed Trainers’ Training on SOS-HOPE (Survival Of Sickest-Handling Office in Paediatric Emergencies), Vadodara, 2016.
  • Participated in Asthma Training Module Workshop (2017) organised by Academy Of Paediatrics, Respiratory Chapter, Vadodara.
  • Successfully completed Post Graduate Program in PEDIATRIC NUTRITION (PGPPN) conferred by Boston University School of Medicine, 2017.
  • Successfully completed Competency-based-approach course: “Antimicrobial Stewardship”, offered by WHO/ Europe, September 2019.

Professional Memberships :

  • Member of Indian Academy of Paediatrics: Central and Vadodara branch.
